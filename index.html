Property Details

Welcome to 56 Machell Avenue

Tucked away on one of Aurora's most peaceful cul-de-sacs, this exceptional property blends historic character, modern luxury and a setting rarely found in newer communities.

Originally built in the 1880s and beautifully renovated, this timeless home is not subject to heritage designation. Sun-filled living spaces, hardwood floors and a gourmet kitchen with premium Bosch and KitchenAid appliances create effortless everyday living.

The primary retreat offers vaulted ceilings, a walk-in closet and a spa-inspired ensuite with heated floors, while three additional bedrooms provide plenty of space for family and guests.

Set on a private 53' x 200' lot backing onto a tranquil stream and Fleury Park, you'll enjoy direct access to tennis and pickleball courts, sports fields, playgrounds and expansive green space—all while being minutes from schools, shopping and everyday amenities.

Freshly painted in July 2026, with a recently waterproofed basement and new sump pump, this rare property offers exceptional privacy, character and lifestyle.
