Property Details

Welcome to 56 Machell Avenue

Tucked away on one of Aurora Village's most coveted cul-de-sacs, this beautifully renovated character home offers a rare combination of privacy, space, and lifestyle that is increasingly difficult to find. 

With 2,438 sq. ft. of above-grade living space, this 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom home sits on an exceptional 53' x 200' lot backing onto a tranquil stream and Fleury Park, creating a private, park-like setting with no homes directly behind. 

Originally built in the 1880s and thoughtfully updated throughout, the home blends timeless character with modern comfort. Sun-filled principal rooms, hardwood flooring, quality finishes, and a spacious layout create an inviting atmosphere for both everyday living and entertaining. The gourmet kitchen features premium stainless-steel appliances, while the impressive primary retreat offers vaulted ceilings, a walk-in closet, and a luxurious 5-piece ensuite with heated floors. 

The backyard is the true showpiece. Mature trees, exceptional privacy, and direct access to parkland create a setting rarely found within walking distance of schools, shops, restaurants, and downtown Aurora. Enjoy nearby tennis and pickleball courts, playgrounds, sports fields, and scenic trails, all just beyond your backyard. 

Freshly painted in 2026, with a recently waterproofed basement and new sump pump, this move-in-ready home offers peace of mind along with an unmatched location. 

A rare opportunity to enjoy country-like tranquility in the heart of Aurora.
